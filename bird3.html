Treatment and Prevention of Depression

If you notice any signs of depression in your bird, be sure to consult your veterinarian. Early identification and treatment of symptoms may drastically increase the lifespan of your birdie. To prevent depression, ensure your bird has a healthy and stimulating environment, and consider providing a companion if appropriate. Regular check-ups with a veterinarian can also help maintain your bird's physical and mental health.
